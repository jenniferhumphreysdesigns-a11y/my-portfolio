About Me

Instructional Designer who blends structure, research, and creative problem-solving to support real-world performance

Hello! I'm Jennifer

I am an Instructional Designer focused on clarity, relevance, and practical outcomes. I design digital and instructor-led learning with an emphasis on strong structure, aligned objectives, and collaboration across teams.

I bring more than a decade of experience supporting learners and facilitators across nonprofit and community-based education settings. That background shapes how I approach analysis, decision-making, and learner support in real-world contexts.

My work begins with a clear understanding of the audience, their goals, constraints, and environment. I use research, data, and existing materials to identify motivation patterns and knowledge gaps, then synthesize learning solutions using evidence-based instructional strategies, simulations, and iterative feedback.
